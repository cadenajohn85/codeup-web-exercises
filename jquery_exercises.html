This page makes use of both English and 日本語.

Japanese has 3 different native scripts:

  • ひらがな (hiragana)
  • カタカナ (katakana)
  • 漢字 (kanji)
You can also write Japanese using English letters, which is called roomaji. Eigo no moji wo tsukatte nihongo wo kaku no ga roomaji to iu koto desu.
